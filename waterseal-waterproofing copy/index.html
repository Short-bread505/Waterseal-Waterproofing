What we do

Waterproofing for every part of your property

From a single leaking shower to a full commercial rooftop, we seal it properly the first time — internal and external, residential and commercial.

Bathrooms & wet areas

Showers, ensuites, laundries and toilets sealed to AS 3740 before tiling — no more hidden leaks behind the wall.

Balconies & decks

Exposed and tiled balconies, podiums and rooftop terraces — trafficable membranes that handle weather and foot traffic.

Roofs & rooftop membranes

Flat and low-pitch roofs, box gutters and parapets coated with durable, UV-stable liquid membrane systems.

Basements & retaining walls

Below-ground tanking and negative-side waterproofing to keep moisture out of basements, garages and cellars.

Planter boxes & podiums

Garden beds, planter boxes and suspended slabs lined to stop water tracking into the structure below.

Remedial & leak repairs

Tracing the real source of a leak and fixing it — re-sealing showers, balconies and failed membranes without the guesswork.

Commercial, strata & new builds welcome — builders and tilers, ask about our trade schedule.

How it works

A straightforward four-step process

No mess, no jargon — just a clear path from first call to a job you can rely on.

  1. 1

    Free inspection & quote

    We come out, assess the area and the cause, then send a clear written quote — no obligation.

  2. 2

    Surface preparation

    The part most people skip. We prep, prime and detail every junction so the membrane bonds properly.

  3. 3

    Membrane application

    The right modern membrane system for the job, applied to the correct thickness and cured properly.

  4. 4

    Flood test & sign-off

    We water-test the work, hand over your documentation, and stand behind it with our guarantee.
